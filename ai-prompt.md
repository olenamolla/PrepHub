## Task
You will be helping with building a full-stack web app called PrepHub. This is the idea of the project: a web app that will help people to prepare for tech interviews by tracking problems they solve. First version or prototype and to deploy need to include such features: users have their space where they can add problems they solved (date, time, ds topic, difficulty level, pattern, notes including their struggle/mistakes/reflection). The app will categorize the problems and will remind users when they need to revisit the problem. It will also count how many times they solved the problem and revisited it, notes history. Users can mark which problems are very easy for them and they do not want to revisit them anymore, or which problems are the hardest and they want to prioritize solving those. Consider all of this just as idea. Your apprentice came up with this project idea and thinks that it is relevant and wants to build it, so real users can use it. apprentice has many feature ideas, but for now, they want to keep it like this until it is deployed, and later would iterate with more features. Keep in mind that your apprentice does not have much prior experience but has a passion and very dedicated to learn.
